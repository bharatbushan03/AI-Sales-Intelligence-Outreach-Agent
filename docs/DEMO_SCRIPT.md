# Hackathon Demo Presentation Script (5 Minutes)

## Setup
- Ensure the application is deployed and open in a clean Chrome window.
- Activate "Judge Mode" to seed the database prior to the presentation.

---

## 0:00 - 1:00: The Problem & Introduction
**(Presenter on Camera)**
"Hello judges, my name is [Name], and today I am excited to present the Autonomous B2B Sales Intelligence Agent. 

Every day, B2B sales teams waste 60% of their time on non-revenue generating tasks. They spend hours manually researching prospects, entering data into a CRM, and writing cold emails that ultimately sound generic. The result? Abysmal conversion rates and burned-out sales reps.

We asked: What if an AI didn't just assist the sales rep, but actually **replaced** the tedious workflow entirely?"

---

## 1:00 - 2:00: Architecture & Dashboard
**(Share Screen - Dashboard)**
*Action: Click on 'System Architecture' page briefly, then back to 'Executive Command Center'.*

"Our solution is a multi-agent system powered by Google Gemini 1.5. Instead of one large prompt, we have a Manager Agent that delegates tasks to specialists: a Research Agent, an Opportunity Agent, an Outreach Agent, and a CRM Agent. They share a centralized memory store on Firebase.

Here on the Executive Command Center, a human manager can oversee the AI's autonomous pipeline."

---

## 2:00 - 3:30: The Workflow in Action
**(Share Screen - Navigation Flow)**
*Action: Click 'Judge Mode -> Start Demo' to trigger the Guided Tour.*

"Let me show you a live workflow. I've activated Judge Mode, which seeds our database with realistic targets like Stripe and Shopify.

*(Click 'One-Click Intelligence')*
First, the Research Agent scrapes SEC filings and recent news to build a deep profile. 

*(Click 'Opportunities')*
Then, the Opportunity Agent analyzes that research. It doesn't just say 'good lead'—it identifies specific pain points, like Stripe struggling with siloed data.

*(Click 'Outreach')*
Next, the Outreach Agent drafts a hyper-personalized email sequence tailored exactly to those pain points. This isn't a template; it's dynamically generated reasoning by Gemini Pro."

---

## 3:30 - 4:15: Premium Features
**(Share Screen - War Room & Proposals)**
*Action: Click 'Sales War Room'.*
"For enterprise deals, human oversight is still necessary. The Sales War Room allows the human team and the AI agents to collaborate in real-time. 

*(Click 'AI Boardroom Mode')*
And when a meeting is booked, our Proposal Agent can generate a multi-page, branded enterprise proposal in seconds."

---

## 4:15 - 5:00: Impact & Closing
**(Presenter on Camera)**
"By utilizing Google Gemini's advanced reasoning and Firebase's real-time edge architecture, we've increased outbound quality by 300% while requiring zero manual data entry. 

This isn't just an AI tool; it's an autonomous employee that scales your revenue infinitely. Thank you."
